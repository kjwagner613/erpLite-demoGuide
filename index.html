Function without the Complexity

Main Menu, when in a module and want to go to another, you have to completely back out to the main menu.

Customer Database

This is the foundation for everything else. Sales, Product Build, Service Orders. Everything starts as a record assigned to a customer.

Shipping and Billing

Each customer needs to have ability to have multiple billing profiles and different shipping addresses. This is done by making shipping and billing separate records, with a relationship of one (customer) to many (shipping addresses or billing profiles) but most have only one of each.

Applied Philosophy

And this single scenario is the cause of so much complexity. Making allowances for times when a customer has multiple, every other user of the software has to master software with deeply layered menus, crazy transaction codes. Headaches. This is an example of the philosophy I put on the website, applied to this one scenario.
